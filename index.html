Green Campus

GU have one of the most green campuses in Assam. In the spring and autumn, the campus wears a very colourful look with its blooming Gulmohurs and other flowering trees like Golden Shower. The university is also famously known as the Land of Gulmohur for the abundance of Golmohur trees throughout the campus. It also figures in popular songs and literatures.

With numerous wet lands and a Botanical Garden, the campus is also a site of local biodiversity.
